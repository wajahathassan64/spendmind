Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpendMind ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, processing, and your rights when using our iOS application ("App").

By using SpendMind, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use SpendMind:

2.1 Voice and Audio Data

  • Microphone Access: The App requires access to your device's microphone to record your voice for expense tracking.
  • Speech Recognition: Your voice recordings are processed locally on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. Audio data is not stored or transmitted to our servers.
  • Transcripts: Speech-to-text transcripts of your voice recordings are created locally and may be sent to OpenAI's API for expense extraction purposes (see Section 5.2).

2.2 Expense Data

  • Expense Records: Amount, currency, date, description, notes, category, and related metadata
  • Transaction Information: Source of expense (voice, manual entry), confidence scores, and timestamps
  • Categories: Custom and default expense categories you create or use

2.3 User Preferences and Settings

  • Language Preferences: Your selected language for speech recognition
  • Currency Settings: Your preferred currency for expense tracking
  • App Settings: Auto-submit transcription preferences, sync settings, and other user-configurable options

2.4 Device Information

  • Device Type: Information about your iOS device
  • App Version: Version of the SpendMind app you are using
  • System Information: iOS version and device capabilities

2.5 CloudKit/iCloud Data (Optional)

  • Sync Data: If you enable iCloud sync, your expense data is stored in Apple's CloudKit service
  • Account Status: iCloud account availability and sync status
  • Note: CloudKit sync is optional and user-controlled. You can disable it at any time in the App settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Core Functionality

  • To provide expense tracking and management services
  • To process voice recordings and extract expense information
  • To store and organize your expense data locally on your device
  • To enable optional cloud synchronization across your devices via iCloud

3.2 Service Improvement

  • To improve the accuracy of expense extraction and categorization
  • To enhance speech recognition capabilities
  • To fix bugs and improve app performance

3.3 Data Export

  • To allow you to export your expense data in CSV or encrypted backup formats
  • To facilitate data portability as required by GDPR and CCPA

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your expense data is primarily stored locally on your iOS device using Core Data. This includes:

  • Expense records and categories
  • User preferences and settings
  • Local database files stored securely in the app's sandbox

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in Apple's CloudKit service. This data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Subject to Apple's iCloud Terms and Conditions
  • Stored in Apple's data centers
  • Accessible only through your Apple ID

4.3 Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • Local data is stored in iOS's secure app sandbox
  • We implement access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • We regularly update the App to address security vulnerabilities
Important: While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

5.1 Apple Services

  • Speech Recognition: We use Apple's Speech Recognition framework, which processes audio locally on your device. Apple's Privacy Policy applies to this service.
  • CloudKit/iCloud: If you enable sync, your data is stored in Apple's CloudKit. Apple's iCloud Terms and Privacy Policy apply.
  • App Store: When you download the App, Apple may collect certain information. Apple's Privacy Policy applies.

5.2 OpenAI API

  • Purpose: We use OpenAI's API to extract structured expense information from voice transcripts
  • Data Sent: Text transcripts of your voice recordings are sent to OpenAI for processing
  • Data Retention: According to OpenAI's API usage policy, API data may be retained for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, then deleted unless you opt out
  • Privacy: OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service apply to data sent to their API
  • Opt-Out: You can contact us to request that we disable OpenAI processing, though this may limit certain App features
Note: We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We only share data with third-party services necessary to provide the App's functionality.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 General Rights (All Users)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data stored in the App
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Export: Export your expense data in CSV or encrypted backup formats
  • Opt-Out: Disable iCloud sync or other optional features

6.2 GDPR Rights (European Union/UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: Obtain confirmation of whether we process your personal data and access to that data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we process your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

6.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

6.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 11 (Contact Information). We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

7. Children's Privacy

SpendMind is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 (or 16 in the EEA) without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

This policy complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country.

8.1 Data Transfer Mechanisms

  • Apple CloudKit: Data may be stored in Apple's data centers worldwide, subject to Apple's data processing agreements
  • OpenAI API: Transcripts may be processed by OpenAI's servers, which may be located outside your country

8.2 Safeguards

We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your data during international transfers, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Use of service providers that comply with international data protection standards

If you are located in the EEA or UK, transfers of your personal data are subject to appropriate safeguards as required by GDPR.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App's services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

9.1 Retention Periods

  • Expense Data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the App
  • Local Storage: Data stored locally on your device remains until you delete the App or clear app data
  • CloudKit Data: Retained until you disable sync or delete your iCloud account
  • OpenAI API Data: Subject to OpenAI's retention policy (typically up to 30 days unless you opt out)

9.2 Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual expense records within the App
  • Exporting and then deleting all data
  • Uninstalling the App (local data will be deleted)
  • Disabling iCloud sync (CloudKit data will be removed)
  • Contacting us to request deletion

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors.

When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you of material changes through the App or via email (if you have provided your email address)
  • Post the updated policy on our website

Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the changes, you should stop using the App and delete your data.
